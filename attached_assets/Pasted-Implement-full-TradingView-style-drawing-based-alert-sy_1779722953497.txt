- Create Alert

- Breakout

- Breakout
- Rejection

PRICE ALERTS:
- Above Price
- Below Price
- Touch Price

==================================================

Using:

- Delta Exchange websocket

2. compare live price
3. detect touches/crosses
4. trigger alerts instantly

TRENDLINE MATH
==================================================

projectedPrice = startPrice + slope * elapsedTime

- future projection
